Leetcode (Problem set)

  • Industry-standard coding interview prep
  • 3000+ problems covering all patterns and difficulty levels
  • Company-specific problem sets from real interviews
  • Discussion forums with multiple solution approaches

Quick Start

  • Create account at leetcode.com
  • Start with "Easy" difficulty problems
  • Do the "Top Interview Questions" collection
  • Read solutions and discussions after attempting
  • Aim for 1-2 problems daily consistently

Neetcode (Job oriented programming courses)

  • Curated list of 150 essential problems (instead of 3000+)
  • Video explanations with clear pattern recognition
  • Roadmap organized by topic difficulty
  • Removes the paralysis of choice

Quick Start

  • Visit neetcode.io for the roadmap
  • Follow the "Blind 75" or "Neetcode 150" list
  • Watch video explanation first, then attempt problem
  • Code along with explanations to understand patterns
  • Subscribe to YouTube channel for free video solutions

Pramp (Mock interview)

  • Free peer-to-peer mock interviews
  • Practice both interviewing and being interviewed
  • Real-time coding environment with video chat
  • Immediate feedback from peers

Quick Start

  • Sign up at pramp.com with your email
  • Schedule your first practice interview
  • Prepare 30 minutes before by reviewing a topic
  • Both interview and be interviewed in same session
  • Review feedback and iterate with more sessions
